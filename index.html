Anatolian rock, or known as Turkish psychedelic rock, is a fusion of Turkish folk music and rock. It emerged during the mid-1960s, soon after rock groups became popular in Turkey.

Most known members of this genre includes Turkish musicians such as Baris Manco, Cem Karaca, Erkin Koray, Selda Bagcan, Fikret Kizilok alongside bands such as Mogollar, Kurtalan Ekspres and 3 Hurel.
